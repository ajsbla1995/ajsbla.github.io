Project 1: Analysis of Wine Scores and Prices

In this project, the relationships of wine scores, prices and descriptions were analyzed in Python and SQL to determine:
1) whether there is strong correlation between price and scores for varietal wines
2) the relative score and price for each varietal wine
3) the frequency of descriptor words by critics for different price and score segments
4) visualizing the relationship of wine scores vs price for wine-growing regions in selected countries

Below is an embedded Tableau dashboard comparing Old and New World wines based on score and price. The user may filter the data based on score, price, varietal, country and province to glean additional insights.
